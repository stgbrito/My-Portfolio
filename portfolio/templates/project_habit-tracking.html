The Habit Tracker is a full-stack web application designed to help users build and maintain positive habits through a simple and engaging interface. The goal of this project was to create a practical tool that provides users with the structure and motivation needed to achieve consistency in their daily routines.

This application solves the common problem of tracking personal growth by moving away from unreliable paper methods. It provides a clear, digital platform to register daily actions, visualize progress over time, and offer the encouragement that comes from seeing a streak of accomplishments.
